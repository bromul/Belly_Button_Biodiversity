About this Project

This page represents the culmination of our work at Improbable Meats to find bacteria within subjects navels that could be synthesized to create a "beef-like" taste in faux-meat.

How to Use this Page

Simply choose a subject's id number from the dropdown menu in the dropdown menu in the top-left corner, and you will see:

(a) Demographic information on the Subject,
(b) The top ten most prolific bacteria we found within their navel,
(c) The number of times the subject washes their navel within a week,
and
(d) A visualization of all bacteria found within the subject's navel, sorted by their OTU Id number.
